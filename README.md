# Privacy Policy for Drunk Town Taxi Co.
Last updated: 08-05-2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Drunk Town Taxi Co. ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
When you play Drunk Town Taxi Co., we may collect the following types of information:

**Gameplay Data:** Non-personal gameplay data, such as high scores, levels completed, taxi fares collected, and ragdoll physics interactions. This helps us balance the game's difficulty.

**Device Information:** Information about your VR headset type (e.g., Quest, Valve Index), operating system, and performance metrics (FPS/Latency). This helps us optimize the physics engine for different hardware.

**VR Sensor Data:** We use standard VR motion tracking (head and hand movement) to enable gameplay. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

**Meta Platform Data (Meta Quest only):** When you play on a Meta Quest device, the game uses the Meta Horizon Platform Services to provide a global leaderboard feature. To enable this, we collect and process the following data fields directly from Meta:

- **User ID:** The unique numeric Oculus User ID assigned to your Meta account. We use this to associate a leaderboard score with your account, prevent duplicate entries, and identify your own entry within the leaderboard so we can highlight your rank.
- **User Profile:** Your Meta/Oculus public display name (username). We use this only to render your name next to your score on the in-game leaderboard so you can recognize yourself and others in the rankings.

This data is provided by Meta under your existing Meta account privacy settings, is stored on Meta's leaderboard service, and is only sent from your Meta Quest device to Meta's servers — it is never sent to any servers controlled by us.

**Analytics:** We use anonymous analytics tools to understand aggregate player behavior. This data is not linked to your Meta User ID or username.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:

- Improve the physics-based driving mechanics of Drunk Town Taxi Co.
- Track and analyze game performance across different VR platforms.
- Fix bugs, specifically those related to ragdoll glitches or VR tracking issues.
- Submit your score to the global Meta Quest leaderboard and display your rank, best score, and username on the in-game Game Over screen and the Meta system leaderboard view.

## 3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal information.

**Meta Platform Services:** Leaderboard scores, your Meta User ID, and your Meta username are processed by Meta Platform Technologies, LLC as part of the Meta Horizon leaderboard service. Use of this service is governed by the [Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.meta.com/legal/privacy-policy/) and your Meta account settings. We do not retain a separate copy of this data on our own servers.

**Legal Obligations:** If required by law, we may share information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

## 4. Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your data and ensure its security. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. Meta Platform Data is secured by Meta in transit and at rest under Meta's own security practices.

## 5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Opt out:** You can opt out of contributing to the global leaderboard by not playing the game, or by uninstalling Drunk Town Taxi Co. from your Meta Quest device.
- **Access and deletion of leaderboard data:** To request that we delete your Meta User ID, Meta username, and associated leaderboard score from our leaderboard records, send an email to **Rushgear49@gmail.com** with the subject line `"DUC Data Deletion Request - Drunk Town Taxi Co."` and include the Meta username under which the score was posted. We will action verified requests within 30 days.
- **Meta-side controls:** You may also manage or delete the data Meta holds about your activity on Meta Quest at any time via your [Meta account settings](https://accounts.meta.com/) and the Meta privacy controls on your headset.
- **Local data:** Locally cached high-score data on your device can be cleared at any time by clearing the application's data through your Meta Quest device's app settings, or by uninstalling the game.

## 6. Children
Drunk Town Taxi Co. is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the minimum age required to hold a Meta account in their region.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time, especially as we move through Early Access. Any changes will be reflected by the date at the top of this page.
